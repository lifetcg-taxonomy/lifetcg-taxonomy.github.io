Evolutionary sequence from earliest animal lineages to most recently derived. Invertebrates anchor the left; Birds (avian dinosaurs) close the right.
How to use this guide. Within each binder, file cards in the proposed order below — earliest lineage first, most recently evolved last. Read each section left to right, column by column. The order groupings serve as natural tab dividers. Card numbers refer to Life TCG set numbers.

Binder 2
Birds
130 species · 19 taxonomic orders
Filing logic for Birds: Palaeognathae (ratites like Emu) come first as the most ancient bird lineage. Galloanserae (fowl, ducks, geese) follow. Then Neoaves — the vast majority — ordered from most ancient to most recently derived lineages. Within families, charismatic or geographically distinctive species are noted.
